How we choose our picks

We don’t run lab tests or time assembly ourselves. Instead, we base our recommendations on years of community feedback, published reviews from trusted ergonomics and tech outlets, and careful analysis of what actually fails over time: motor wobble under load, controller glitches, and laminate peeling on lower‑priced tops. We also track long‑term owner reports on forums and social media to spot reliability trends that reviews written after a week miss. A desk earns a spot on this list only if it shows consistently high owner satisfaction across thousands of units, stands up to daily sit‑stand cycles, and offers a warranty and customer support record that gives us confidence. We also weigh practical factors like shipping damage rates, replacement part availability, and how much adjustment range really works for tall or short users. Every pick is a balance of stability, durability, customization, and genuine value, not just a list of specs.
